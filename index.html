About Me

I am a passionate undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, pursuing a B.Tech. in Engineering Physics with minors in Mathematics and Computing. My driving interest lies at the intersection of experimental high-energy physics and its innovative applications with machine learning. My research portfolio spans developing Graph Neural Networks for the CMS experiment, firmware development for the ATLAS experiment's readout chain, and in-depth heavy ion physics analysis. I am fueled by intellectual curiosity and a commitment to contributing to impactful scientific discoveries.
