About Me

       
           

Undergraduate, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, B.Tech Engineering Physics with minors in Mathematics and Computing. Research focuses on the intersection of **Experimental High-Energy Physics (HEP)** and **Machine Learning (ML)**. Expertise includes developing **Graph Neural Networks (GNN)** for physics object reconstruction in the **CMS experiment** at CERN and contributing to **ATLAS detector** firmware development. Driven by fundamental scientific inquiry and advanced computational methods to derive novel insights from large-scale particle collider data.

Speaker and LED Composite System

               

                    Designed a sound-reactive light show system for the Analog Systems course. The circuit processes an audio signal to drive both a speaker and synchronized LEDs. Implemented a DC-DC buck converter, active band-pass filters (bass, mid, treble), peak detectors, and a high-efficiency Class-D audio amplifier. All stages were rigorously simulated in **LTSpice** and achieved the highest project grade.                

               

Technologies: LTSpice, Circuit Design, PCB Layout (KiCAD), Class-D Amplifiers, Active Filters
